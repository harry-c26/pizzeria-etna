Welcome to Pizzeria Etna, Águilas.


Pizzeria Etna is a family run Italian Restaurant in Águilas. We pride ourselves on the quality of our pizzas and this comes from using fresh ingredients imported from Italy.

Combining the fresh ingredients and the authenticity that the Sicilian chef brings to the pizza oven has led to Pizzeria Etna being recognised for the Best Pizza in Águilas by Tripadvisor in 2021.

This recognition was followed in 2022 with a place on the Travellers Choice list and remaining the number 1 ranked Pizzeria in town.
